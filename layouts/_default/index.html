About Us

Beauty is our passion, your confidence is our reward

At Palette Co, we specialise in luxurious lash extensions, lash lifts, brow lamination and facials that enhance your natural beauty and elevate your look. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming and comfortable atmosphere where you can relax and let us work our magic. Whether you are preparing for a special occasion or simply want to enhance your everyday look, our goal is to make you feel pampered and cherished.

Located at Salon Lane Factory in West End, Brisbane — join us at Palette Co, where every set of lashes is a work of art, and discover how we can help you express your unique beauty.
